Shopping Guide:


Please follow the steps below to sign up for a purchase on the Kleider website:



Step 1: AccessAccess to the website Kleider to find the items you want to buy in the following ways:

  • Using the search engine, type the name of the product you want to buy
  • View Kleider products
  • Refer to "New Product"
  • Refer to "Best Seller"
  • Refer to "Design Style"


Step 2: Cart

  • Click on the button "Add to cart" to add item into your Shopping Cart
  • Note: Make sure you loged in before adding item into your shopping cart



Step 3: Shipping

  • Click on the button "Buy" and you will access Step Shipping
  • Here, you are able to estimate your Shipping fee before paying
